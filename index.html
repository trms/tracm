About

Tracm is a client for the ACM Shared Content Server and other content sharing sites.

Usage

Use the settings tab to enter your credentials for the ACM Shared Content Server and select a download folder. After adding videos to your download queue, click the download tab and click "Refresh SCS Queue" to download the video files to your download folder. That's it!

You can use the Upload tab to enter metadata and queue a video file to be uploaded under your account to the Shared Content Server.

If you have a Cablecast server*, you can enter the settings for it on the Settings tab and tracm will automatically fetch the metadata from Cablecast for files you're uploading. Furthermore, tracm will create a show record in Cablecast after downloading any video files as well.

* Cablecast integration requires Cablecast 4.9
